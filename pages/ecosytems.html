Ecosystems

"Ecosystems" are a group of elements that revolve around one main crypto or COIN, in which other "coins" otherwise known as tokens are built off of such as meme coins, they are built off of ETH. There are many types of "Ecosystems" and they all have supporting elements to them. Every major coin has its own unique use cases driving its growth, ETH for one has many uses to access other assets on the market like NFT's, and other coins apart of ETH's "Ecosystem", whereas Bitcoin's use is of long-term investment returns, which at the rate of ETH, will also be the case, ETH will be the currency of innovation in the coming years. These are apart of the DeFi platforms, which have been growing in value since last year tremendously and continuing. Stablecoins are the most frequent when it comes to being traded, being made as a sort of settlement system, it is a source of stable storage for traders.
