My Opinion on what to Invest in

Stable coins, are the least riskiest, and have high return rates, which would sound too good to be true but many have put high inputs of money into them and have gotten returns in a week. These stable coins have a highly complex system that even if the market where to crash, your stable coin will have the least risk of losing all money. Stable coins such as DAI and Olympus Dao are personally the best options, with Olympus Dao having many different options, having regulated APY returns.
Even just having ETH to keep overtime is a good option as with the current yearly growth rates of ETH, compared to Bitcoin, there will be a continuing rise in the value of crypto. Whether you get a small piece of Bitcoin which investors aim at growing even more or ETH, the rise of the concept of the Metaverse is coming soon, where soon the value of crypto will rise more as more valuables will be purchased with crypto assets.
Especially now that Bidens new Infrastructure Bill is being passed, the inflation rates will soon rise, and the value of the USD will decline, but with your money invested in crypto, that will not affect the value, in fact making crypto even more valuable as the price of the USD changes, but could affect the stock market greatly.
