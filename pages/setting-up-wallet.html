Making an account on MetaMask

MetaMask, a crypto wallet service that you will be able to make, this is where you are able to store any crypto onto and are able to connect to Coinbase when making exchanges. This will be important as the wallet is what stores your crypto and will be used to connect to almost all other parts of the market in crypto, the gateway to other projects, and blockchain apps. This can also be used to buy NFT's with. This wallet is specifically used with ETH.
