Stocks

Once you own stocks, you are now a shareholder of the corporation you invested in. This category of asset exchanges are apart of Centralized companies and corporations. Market makers form the backbone of all trading and provide the liquidity(the availability of liquid assets to a market, basically the company's ability to raise cash when it needs it.) of stocks. There are many types of stocks to invest in and have different pros and cons to long term vs short term, whether you invest into indexes that handle your money and place them into different corporations, or singular investing which is you investing into companies/corporations yourself.
